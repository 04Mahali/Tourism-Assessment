About the Event

The Lesotho Cultural Festival started in 2018 to promote Basotho culture and tourism.
The Lesotho Cultural Festival is an annual event that celebrates the rich traditions and heritage of Basotho.
It gives visitors an opportunity to experience the country's culture, lifestyle, and hospitality.
It brings together traditional dancers, artists, chefs, and visitors from different countries.
Lesotho Cultural Festival supports local businesses and artists by providing a platform to display and sell their products.

Target Tourists

  • International Tourists
  • Local Visitors & families
  • Cultural Explorers
  • Students and researchers interested in heritage tourism
